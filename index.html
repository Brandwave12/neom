ONTEXT is a Belgium-based multinational company, known for its global reach and remarkable revenue of _ million dollars. Ontex is a global leader in personal hygiene, providing high-quality products for Baby Care, Feminine Care, and Adult Care. Trusted by consumers, retailers, and healthcare providers worldwide, Ontex is the partner of choice for quality and reliability.

NEOM World Trade Pvt. Ltd., operating under the Pathivara Group, is the authorized distributor of Ontex in Nepal. With a strong presence across all seven provinces of the country, Neom specializes in distributing high-quality baby care and cosmetic products, with adult care solutions set to launch soon. Committed to excellence, Neom ensures that every product meets the highest standards of quality and hygiene, making it a trusted name for consumers nationwide. Its extensive distribution network and dedication to customer satisfaction have solidified its position as a leading player in the market.
