Pathivara Groups: Leading with Vision and Purpose

Pathivara World Trade is a Nepal-based company with a diverse portfolio spanning multiple sectors, including hospitality, hardware supply, AI, and innovation. Known for its commitment to quality and reliability, Pathivara World Trade is dedicated to delivering excellence both locally and internationally. We strive to meet the evolving needs of our customers by continuously expanding our services and driving progress through innovation. With a focus on enhancing lives and creating value, Pathivara World Trade has become a trusted name across industries.

Under the Pathivara World Trade umbrella, we proudly manage four key organizations: Neom World Limited, a leader in delivering world-class solutions; Ontex, known for its premium-quality products; and divisions specializing in hospitality and AI technology. Together, these entities share a commitment to innovation, sustainability, and positive community impact, working towards a brighter future for all.

Hilltone Restaurant & Family Restro

Hilltone Restaurant & Family Restro is dedicated to providing a wholesome dining experience with a focus on hygienic, high-quality food. From the very beginning, we have been committed to creating a space where families and friends can gather and enjoy delicious meals in a clean, safe environment. Our dedication to maintaining the highest standards of cleanliness and food safety ensures that every dish served is both tasty and healthy.

Hilltone Restaurant & Family Restro operates in key locations including Biratnagar, Dudh Farm, Morang, and more, offering a wide variety of dishes that cater to different tastes and preferences. Our restaurants provide a warm, welcoming atmosphere perfect for family outings, gatherings, and celebrations. With every visit, we aim to create lasting memories while ensuring your dining experience is safe and enjoyable.

Pathivara Hardware & Suppliers

Pathivara Hardware & Suppliers is a trusted name in providing high-quality hardware products and solutions across Nepal. Our company is dedicated to offering a wide range of products, from essential building materials to specialized tools, catering to both personal and industrial needs. With a focus on reliability and customer satisfaction, we ensure that every product meets the highest standards of quality and durability.

Our operations extend across various locations, including Taplejung, Pathivara, and other parts of Nepal, ensuring that our customers have easy access to the supplies they need. Whether you are in need of plumbing materials, electrical components, or construction tools, Pathivara Hardware & Suppliers is your go-to source for quality hardware and timely delivery. We are proud to serve communities in Taplejung, Pathivara, and beyond, delivering excellence and reliability with every order.
