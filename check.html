It means making sure our people commitments are progressive - so we can continue to keep our employees safe at work and help them be the best they can be. Awareness Against social Taboo

Our mission is to eradicate Chhaupadi Pratha by fostering awareness, empowering women, and advancing gender equality through education, healthcare initiatives, and community-centered solutions. We are committed to creating safe and inclusive spaces where women and girls can live with dignity, breaking the cycle of stigma and inequality associated with menstruation, and promoting a culture of respect and acceptance.

Equality, Inclusion, and Diversity

We are dedicated to fostering openness, inclusivity, and the celebration of diversity in every facet of our operations. This commitment is reflected in our practices, from hiring individuals with diverse backgrounds, beliefs, and abilities to ensuring that our marketing materials authentically represent the vibrant diversity of our community.

"Diversity is the heart of progress, and inclusivity is the path to unity. By embracing every voice and every story, we create a community where everyone belongs."

"A brighter future begins with how we care for and empower our children today. By championing their rights, we shape a world where every child has the chance to thrive."

Children’s Rights

Children are at the heart of everything we do. Operating our business responsibly with a strong focus on their well-being has always been a core value for us. Guided by Save Children’s Rights and Business Principles, we ensure that every aspect of our operations—from production to marketing and distribution—champions the rights and dignity of children. These principles serve as our playbook, shaping how we integrate the protection and empowerment of children into our daily practices. By adhering to these values, we’re not just making statements; we’re taking meaningful action to build a future that genuinely prioritizes the well-being, growth, and opportunities for children in Nepal.

Education for Every child

We are steadfast in our mission to create equal educational opportunities for every child in Nepal, regardless of their socio-economic background, geographic location, or personal circumstances. Education is the cornerstone of progress, and we envision a future where every child in Nepal not only completes secondary school but does so with a robust foundation of knowledge, critical thinking skills, and confidence.

Our aim is to empower children to pursue higher education and specialized fields of their choice, enabling them to achieve their aspirations and compete successfully in the global workforce. We believe that when children are given the tools to excel, they not only transform their own lives but also contribute to the sustainable growth and development of their communities and the nation as a whole.

Through this commitment, we strive to bridge gaps in access, quality, and opportunity, ensuring that education becomes a universal right rather than a privilege. We work tirelessly to create an inclusive, supportive, and equitable learning environment where every child has the opportunity to unlock their full potential and build a brighter future.
